6. Billing & Policy FAQ

Rollover Policy

Unused tokens expire at the end of your billing month. We operate on a "Use It or Lose It" basis to maintain our low infrastructure pricing. Tokens purchased via Top-Up Packs do not expire as long as you have an active subscription.

Overage Protection

We never auto-charge your card. If your balance hits zero, your data streams simply pause. You can enable "Auto-Recharge" in your dashboard if you prefer uninterrupted service.

Anonymous Payments

We support privacy-first organizations. You may pay for any tier or token pack using cryptocurrencies or other Payment Methods (Credit/Debit Card, Google and Apple Pay etc. all anonymized) via our licensed third-party processors. No personal billing address required.

Refunds

Due to the digital nature of tokens and the irreversibility of crypto transactions, all sales are final. We offer a Free Tier so you can validate our data quality before committing financially.
